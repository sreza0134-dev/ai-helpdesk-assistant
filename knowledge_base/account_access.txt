Title: Granting Access to Shared Drives or Folders

If a user requests access to a shared drive, folder, or team resource:
1. Confirm the user's full name, employee ID, and which team or department they belong to.
2. Verify with the user's manager or team lead that they should have this access.
3. Do not grant access without manager confirmation for sensitive or restricted folders.
4. Once approved, grant the minimum level of access needed (read-only vs edit, if applicable).
5. Document the access change with date, approver name, and reason.
