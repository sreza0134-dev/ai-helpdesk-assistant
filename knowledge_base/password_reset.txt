Title: Password Reset Procedure

If a user is locked out of their account or forgot their password:
1. Verify the user's identity by confirming their full name and employee ID.
2. Do not reset a password without identity verification, even if the request seems urgent.
3. Send a password reset link to the user's registered recovery email or phone number.
4. Advise the user to choose a password with at least 12 characters, including numbers and symbols.
5. If the user cannot verify their identity through standard means, escalate to the security team.
